# Journey Map

**Layer 1 · Lifecycle Map · Template 02**

The journey map is the experience view: what the person the service is for does as they move through it, stage by stage, and how it feels from their side. It answers "what is it like to go through this?" The machinery behind the experience lives in the service blueprint (03); this template stays on the person's side of the line.

Build it mostly from interviews and from watching the work happen. Map the real journey, detours and all, not the intended one. AI can put together a first draft of the stage blocks from the tagged interviews, but treat it as a rough sketch to rebuild, not a map to sign: every line traces to something a person actually said, and their own words stay as they said them.
